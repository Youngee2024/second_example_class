What We Do

Accident and Illness

Our services cover for accidents, including broken bones, cuts, and ingestion of foreign objects. Also illness such as cancer, arthritis, allergies, and infections.

Surgery and Hospitalization

Our services covers for surgical procedures, including emergency surgeries and non-emergency procedures. Also hospitalization and extended stays at the veterinary hospital.

Medication and Test

Our services covers for prescription medications, including antibiotics, pain medication, and other necessary medications. Also diagnostic test such as X-rays, blood tests, and ultrasounds.

Hereditary Condition and Therapies

Our services covers for genetic or hereditary conditions that may affect your pet's health. Also alternative therapies such as acupuncture, physical therapy, and chiropractic care.
